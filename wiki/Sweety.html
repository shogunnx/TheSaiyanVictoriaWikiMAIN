Her reputation as well use to heavily revolve around as the shortest fuse of conton city due to her imstability to control her power and... being caught in her firestorm of fist, feet and fury. Now she can be seen as truly herself and builds towards good relations, but from time to time finds herself in a hilarious situation.

When She was but only nine years of age, her older brother Gubber was teaching her the signature candy beam on random targets before the one called Veiss and his army conquered the eastern quadrant as well their own world. In a dare to see if she'd actually do it, the dare was to turn their own sun, into a marshmellow. Not one at the time to back down, she boldly turned the rare blue sun known as Bila into that of a everyday marshmellow and furthermore ate it. For what came next was entirely life altering for that suns aptitude and power was both destroying her very being and yet recreating her all at once, through numerous deaths and rebirths that even the smartests of residents on planet Bila had no solution too. Only Gubber knows the truth of these events and rarely speaks of it without hurting both himself as well sweety who both miss her to this day.
