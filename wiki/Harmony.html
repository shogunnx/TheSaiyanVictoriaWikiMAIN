Harmony

Harmony – The Fusion of the Three Sisters

Overview

Harmony is the youngest and most promising fighter of the sisters. She is a fusion of the spirits of Victoria Chaser, Victoria Black, and Wargirl. While less experienced, Harmony’s potential is limitless, making her a key figure in the Xenoverse storylines.

She is considered Victoria Black’s “daughter,” though in reality, she embodies the combined legacy of all three sisters.

Appearance

  • Height: 5'4"
  • Weight: ~120 lbs
  • Complexion: Light brown, similar to Zendaya’s skin tone
  • Hair: Long golden Super Saiyan 3 hair, sometimes worn in a ponytail
  • Eyes: Teal
  • Casual Outfit:
    • Black Majuub-style fusion jacket
    • Black shorts
    • White and gold low-cut Adidas shoes
    • Gold bracelets
    • Headphones around her neck or ears

Personality

  • Serious, resourceful, and tech-savvy
  • Often more mature in tense moments, though still youthful and curious
  • Has deep loyalty to her sisters, especially Victoria Black
  • Bold enough to act independently, but insecure about measuring up to Binary’s raw power
  • Wary of Binary’s destructive nature, but still tries to reach her

Abilities

  • Fusion Potential – Harmony embodies the combined strengths of all three sisters
  • Technology Genius – Inventor of devices such as the pen-shaped Dice3
  • Super Saiyan 3 Transformation – Though young, she can transform into SSJ3 with long golden hair and massive power increase
  • Sensing Energy – Strong intuition; can detect her mother’s presence through music themes and distorted ki signals
  • Combat Skills – Fast, precise, and strategic in battle

Relationships

  • Victoria Black – Seen as her “mother,” Harmony admires her strength and beauty
  • Victoria Chaser – Her honorable spirit contributes to Harmony’s conscience
  • Wargirl – Younger-sister bond; Harmony looks up to her fighting prowess
  • Binary – Rival. Binary mocks her as a “goodie two shoes,” but Harmony fears her destructive power
  • Goten and Kid Trunks – Allies and friends; they fought alongside her during the Gas Mask Clan rescue

Key Story Events

Identity

Permanent spirit fusion of the three sisters, often called Harmony Blaster.

Upbringing Conflict

Caught between Victoria Chaser’s “earn it” discipline and Victoria Black’s protective shielding; Kai advocates rigorous training with safeguards.

Partnerships

Trains closely with Wargirl; pivotal in the Turles energy recovery.

  • Gas Mask Clan Rescue – Tracks the lair, leads Goten and Trunks to save Wargirl, carries her out while Gotenks holds off the clan
  • Victoria Chaser’s Sacrifice – Witnesses her mother’s sister sacrifice herself during the rescue mission
  • Mall Transformation – Transforms into SSJ3 after seeing Turles, only to be blackmailed by Binary
  • Bank Confrontation – Partners with Binary against criminals, only to face Evil Victoria and Merged Zamasu

Trivia

  • Harmony usually wears headphones and listens to music, even in casual moments
  • Despite being younger and shorter (5’4”), she has the greatest potential of the sisters
  • Her black fusion jacket and Adidas shoes are key parts of her look and should always be shown in depictions
