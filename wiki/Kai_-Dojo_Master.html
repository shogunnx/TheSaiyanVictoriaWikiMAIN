Kai (Dojo Master)

'Canon status: Prime Canon''

Overview

Kai is the brother of Victoria Chaser, Victoria Black, and Wargirl. A disciplined dojo master, he anchors the family’s training ethos after they flee their doomed future and arrive in the past.

Personality

Composed, methodical, protective. Believes in mastery through repetition, restraint, and composure under pressure. Stabilizer among Chaser’s honor, Black’s boldness, and Wargirl’s combat aggression.

Abilities

Dojo Mastery: stances, footwork, conditioning, sparring systems. Instructional Ki Control: precision, conservation, timed releases. Tactical Programs: opponent-specific drills scaling toward elite threats.

Notable Roles

Prologue Era: establishes structure and recovery in the family’s training. Philosophy Mediator: advocates rigorous training with guardrails for Harmony. Turles Thread (support): his regimens underpin the readiness that enables Binary’s restoration and Turles’s defeat.

Relationships

Victoria Chaser: respects her discipline; tempers “hard way only” with smart recovery. Victoria Black: channels her power into control and decision discipline. Wargirl: builds endurance and matchup IQ to fuel her PvP drive. Harmony: promotes a balanced path—earnest work without over‑sheltering.
