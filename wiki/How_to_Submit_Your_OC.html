How to Submit Your OC

We welcome viewer OCs. There are no strict combat requirements; selections are random, and appearances may vary by arc.

How to submit

Post your OC using the template below on the Community page or share via comments under the latest YouTube video. Linking art is okay; credit your artist and confirm usage permissions.
