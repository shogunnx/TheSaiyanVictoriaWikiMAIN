Trakeena

Overview

Trakeena is a rising antagonist in the TheSaiyanVictoria Universe. A powerful Majin empress, she cloaks her ambitions behind a façade of regal composure. Her true goal is to capture Veronica, seeking to extract and harness the source of Veronica’s unique scientific powers for her own empire.

Appearance

Trakeena embodies the classic Majin form, with smooth pink skin, sharp feminine curves, and elegant attire that fuses royal empress garb with the elastic adaptability of her Majin body. Her crown-like headpiece and flowing robes mark her status, while her aura radiates both majesty and menace.

Personality

Coldly ambitious and calculating, Trakeena is as much a strategist as she is a warrior. She does not waste energy in needless displays of strength; instead, she prefers to manipulate and scheme, letting others fall into her traps. Beneath her elegance lies a ruthless hunger for control over knowledge and power.

Powers and Abilities

  • Majin Physiology – Elastic, regenerative body with high resistance to physical damage.
  • Absorption Arts – Ability to absorb knowledge or energy by engulfing a target, leaving them drained.
  • Psionic Command – Projects her will into subordinates, binding weaker minds to her orders.
  • Science Seeker – Unique obsession with harnessing Veronica’s engineering genius; rumored to be developing Majin–machine hybrids.

Goals

Trakeena’s central objective is the capture of Veronica, believing the scientist’s inventions and hidden energy source will ensure the eternal reign of the Majin Dominion.

Role in the Story

As an upcoming villainess, Trakeena will emerge as a direct foil to Veronica — not seeking to destroy her, but to exploit her mind and essence. This makes her an unusually cerebral Majin antagonist, more dangerous for her cunning than for sheer strength.
