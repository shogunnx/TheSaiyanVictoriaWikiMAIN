Gas Mask Clan Saga

== Overview == The Gas Mask Clan kidnaps Wargirl, drawing the team into a rescue operation. Harmony and Gotenks attempt a save that spirals into a larger confrontation. Victoria Chaser consumes her final potion to become Victoria Blue one last time, secures the rescue, and dies after the transformation ends.

Key Characters

Wargirl — captive turned survivor Harmony — pivotal in rescue Victoria Chaser (Victoria Blue) — final transformation and sacrifice Antagonists — Gas Mask Clan (leaders and lieutenants TBD)

Plot Summary

Setup — Wargirl is captured; Harmony and allies mobilize. Conflict — Assault on the Clan’s lair; escalating fights; time pressure. Climax — Victoria Chaser becomes Victoria Blue using her last potion to overpower the enemy and save Wargirl. Outcome — Rescue succeeds; Chaser dies as the potion wears off, ending Victoria Blue permanently unless a new source is found.

Consequences

Permanent end to Victoria Blue (no new potion supply). Wargirl’s recovery arc and renewed rivalries. Harmony’s growing role.
