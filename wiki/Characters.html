Characters

Characters of the The Saiyan Victoria's Universe

Welcome to the **character index** of the SaiyanVictoria Lore Wiki! Here you’ll find every original character, organized by their role in the story. Click any name to view their full profile

Community Creations — a collection of original characters (OCs) donated by fans of the Saiyan Victoria Universe.

👑 Main Sisters

  • Victoria Black – Seductive, manipulative, and powerful; evolves into Binary.
  • Victoria Chaser – The honorable twin sister; later becomes Victoria Blue.
  • Wargirl – The youngest sister; bottom-heavy fighter with glowing pink eyes.

💎 Transformations & Evolutions

  • Binary – The destructive winged evolution of Victoria Black.
  • Harmony – Fusion daughter, spirit of all three sisters combined.

🔥 Other Core Characters

  • Vanessa – Playful and bold; appears in multiple forms.
    • Goddess Vanessa – Red and gold God of Destruction form.
    • Swordswoman Vanessa – Pinup warrior with the Janemba sword.
    • Succubus Vanessa — A seductive demonic alter ego of Vanessa, who enslaves powerful male warriors through her soul-binding ritual. Surrounded by explosive ghost minions and driven by lust and pride, she turns worthy survivors into her fiercely protective "cast."
  • Veronica - The human who saved WarGirl during the Broly raid]]
  • FlashDancer – Stylish cousin, often fusing with Vanessa.
  • DiscoGirl – Wargirl’s groovy alter ego.
  • Evil Victoria – Silver-haired alter ego born from Zamasu’s deal.
  • The Past Warrior – An alternate version of Drakor, a powerful ancient Saiyan permanently locked in Super Saiyan 3 with his warrior’s blade.
  • Cellegsus – Hybrid Cell-inspired design, calm and serious.
  • Trakeena – Majin Empress and emerging adversary seeking Veronica’s power

🌀 Fusions

  • Aeria Destroyer – Fusion of Vanessa and FlashDancer.
  • Gotenks – (support) Fusion of Goten and Kid Trunks, ally of Harmony.

🧑‍🏫 Supporting & Side Characters

  • Drakor – Rival to Victoria Black in PvP battles.
  • Savage Jay – (future arc) streetwise fighter.
  • Shaggy – Reality-breaking powerhouse.
  • Hit – Assassin who tests the sisters in combat.
  • Cell Max – Threat in later arcs.
