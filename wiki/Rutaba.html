Rutaba

Rutaba (nicknamed Ruta) is a Saiyan fighter in the Saiyan Victoria Universe.

Powers and Abilities

  • Bomba – A unique hybrid technique combining a fireball and an energy blast.
  • Kamehameha – Classic ki wave technique mastered by Rutaba.
  • Bomba Kamehameha – A devastating fusion of the Kamehameha with the explosive energy of the Bomba.
