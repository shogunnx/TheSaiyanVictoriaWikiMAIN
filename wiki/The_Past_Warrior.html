The Past Warrior

Appearance

The Past Warrior is an ancient Saiyan locked in his Super Saiyan 3 state. He has long golden spiked hair that flows past his shoulders, a thick beard, and a serious, battle-worn expression. His outfit consists of dark warrior armor with shoulder plates, a tan scarf, leather gloves, and a utility belt with pouches. He carries a sword across his back, a constant reminder of his warrior lineage.

Personality

Stoic and disciplined, The Past Warrior represents the lost age of Saiyan honor. Unlike modern Saiyans, his demeanor is somber, with a deep sense of responsibility for protecting those around him. He speaks little but commands respect through his presence and power.

Powers and Abilities

  • Ancient Super Saiyan 3 – Permanently maintains SSJ3, having mastered the form beyond modern Saiyans. His stamina drain is nearly nonexistent compared to current generations.
  • Warrior’s Blade – A heavy sword strapped to his back. Though rarely used, when drawn it channels his ki to cut through magical and ki-based defenses.
  • Saiyan Heritage – Possesses amplified strength, durability, and battle sense inherited from the ancient Saiyans, making him a formidable strategist as well as a fighter.
  • Temporal Resilience – As a being pulled from the past, his ki reacts differently to time-based attacks, granting partial resistance to distortions and manipulations.

History

The Past Warrior is believed to be a displaced alternate version of Drakor, brought forward from a forgotten era of Saiyan battles. His survival into the present day suggests powerful temporal interference or deliberate summoning.
